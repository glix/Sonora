Sprinkler Repair, Installation, and Repiping Services

In recent years, Sonora Sprinkler has led in the effort to develop solutions for deteriorating polyethylene drip systems. We specialize in repiping poly hose drip systems with rigid pvc, a durable alternative to thin-wall pipe. Through our careful handling of the existing landscape, we are able to install irrigation systems without compromising the curb appeal of your home, restoring the landscape to the exact former appearance.

Other projects we work with include system remodel/revamp, wire tracing/valve locating, hydraulic system troubleshooting, leak detection, & solutions for water conservation.
