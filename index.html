Sprinkler Repair and Repiping Services Is

Daren Jantz, the owner of Sonora Sprinkler Inc., founded the company in 1995 as a small irrigation repair business. With his success through personalized service & commitment to customer satisfaction, the company has grown, continuing to provide premium service & a professional business experience.

We're now the go-to company for sprinkler system repair, installation, and rigid PVC repiping services in Sun City, Sun City West, Surprise, Goodyear, and Peoria, Arizona. Geared for efficiency, our technicians do system diagnostics and implement effective, long-term solutions, relieving homeowners the headache of tackling difficult water problems themselves.
